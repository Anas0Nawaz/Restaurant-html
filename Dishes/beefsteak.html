Beef Steak

Beef steak is a popular dish made from beef, typically a cut that is tender enough for quick cooking methods such as grilling, pan-searing, or broiling. It's enjoyed worldwide for its rich flavor and versatility.Different cuts of beef steaks include ribeye, sirloin, filet mignon, and T-bone, each offering a unique texture and taste experience. Cooking preferences vary from rare to well-done, depending on personal taste and cultural traditions. Pairing with side dishes like mashed potatoes, vegetables, or salad complements the steak's savory profile.
