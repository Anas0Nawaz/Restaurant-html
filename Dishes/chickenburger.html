Chicken Burger

A chicken burger is a popular fast-food item consisting of a breaded or grilled chicken patty served in a soft hamburger bun. The chicken patty is typically seasoned with various spices and herbs to enhance its flavor. It is often accompanied by toppings such as lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and cheese, as well as condiments like mayonnaise, ketchup, or mustard. Chicken burgers are known for their savory taste and are a convenient and satisfying option for a quick meal. They can be found on the menus of fast-food chains and restaurants worldwide, offering a delicious alternative to traditional beef burgers.
