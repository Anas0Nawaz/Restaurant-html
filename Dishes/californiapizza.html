California Pizza

California pizza is a style of pizza known for its unique and innovative toppings that reflect the diverse culinary influences of California cuisine. It typically features a thin crust topped with non-traditional ingredients such as barbecue chicken, avocado, sun-dried tomatoes, artichoke hearts, and a variety of fresh vegetables. California pizza often incorporates flavors from Mediterranean, Mexican, and Asian cuisines, offering a fusion of tastes that appeal to a wide range of palates. It is characterized by its creativity and use of fresh, locally sourced ingredients, making it a popular choice for pizza enthusiasts looking for something beyond the traditional toppings.
