Macroni

Macaroni is a type of pasta characterized by its short, hollow, and cylindrical shape. It is commonly used in a variety of dishes, including pasta salads, casseroles, and as a base for sauces like macaroni and cheese. Macaroni is typically made from durum wheat and is boiled until it becomes tender but still firm to the bite. It is versatile and pairs well with a wide range of ingredients, making it a popular choice for quick and satisfying meals.
